Two players, one camera. Two marionettes stand side by side; each hand drives the puppet on its side of the screen. Each fingertip is a string — five numbered control points follow your fingers and each pulls one body part.

The binding mirrors per hand so the strings never cross: for the hand on the right of screen, 1 thumb → screen-left hand · 2 index → screen-left foot · 3 middle → head · 4 ring → screen-right foot · 5 pinky → screen-right hand; the other hand's binding is the left/right mirror.

Move your whole hand to move the puppet; spread or curl individual fingers to work the limbs. Keep each hand on its own half. The camera overlay (bottom-right) rings both hands' driving fingertips in matching colours. The weight + drag sliders apply to both puppets.
