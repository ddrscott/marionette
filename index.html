No control bar anymore — each fingertip is a string. The five numbered control points follow your fingers and each pulls one body part:

1 thumb → left hand · 2 index → left foot · 3 middle → head · 4 ring → right foot · 5 pinky → right hand.

Move your whole hand to move the puppet; spread or curl individual fingers to work the limbs. The camera overlay (bottom-right) rings the five driving fingertips in matching colours.

The question: with one string per finger, can you puppeteer with intent — or is the double-indirection too much? (Finger→part bindings will be editable later.)
